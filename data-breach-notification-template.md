# GDPR Data Breach Notification Template

**Version:** 1.0  
**Last Updated:** July 2026  
**Template by:** TrustGrid Technology Limited  

---

> **How to use this template:**
> This template contains two notification letters.
> Letter A is for notifying the supervisory authority of a data breach.
> Letter B is for notifying affected individuals.
> Replace all text in square brackets with your specific information.
> Supervisory authorities must be notified within 72 hours of becoming aware of a breach.

---

[List the specific data categories affected]

**What we have done**

[List the actions taken to contain the breach and protect affected individuals]

**What you should do**

We recommend you take the following steps to protect yourself:

- Monitor your bank accounts and financial statements for any unusual activity
- Be alert to unsolicited emails, calls, or messages asking for personal information or passwords
- Change your password on our platform and on any other platforms where you use the same password

- Contact us immediately if you notice any suspicious activity related to your account

## Breach Notification Checklist

- [ ] Breach contained and initial investigation complete
- [ ] Breach reference number assigned
- [ ] Severity assessment completed
- [ ] Supervisory authority notified within 72 hours where required
- [ ] Supervisory authority notification logged with date and time sent
- [ ] Assessment of whether individual notification is required completed
- [ ] Affected individuals identified where possible
- [ ] Individual notifications prepared and sent where required
- [ ] Individual notifications logged
- [ ] Breach entered in breach register
- [ ] Legal counsel notified where appropriate
- [ ] Senior management briefed
- [ ] Post-breach review scheduled

---

## When Must You Notify?

| Who | When | Threshold |
|---|---|---|
| Supervisory Authority | Within 72 hours of becoming aware | Where breach is likely to result in a risk to individuals |
| Affected Individuals | Without undue delay | Where breach is likely to result in a high risk to individuals |

If notification to the supervisory authority is made after 72 hours, the reasons for the delay must be provided.
